About Us

Employ_A is a full scale HR cloud computing software focused on the management of employees data and other request as regarding human resources operation. It handles employee profiling, recruitment management. on-boarding payroll, staff relations, clock-Ins, vacation requests, compensations, benefits and so on.

Our Mission: Developing an enterprise-wide human resource information software for managing employees data, requests and other human resources operations for managers across the globe.

Our VIsion: Reshaping and changing the traditional way of employee management to a more tech focused modern future of HR management.

OUR VALUES: Committed towards innovation, excellence, integrity and creating a results-driven culture that builds teamwork and secures vital information for easy access.
